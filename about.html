Welcome to My Personal Page!

Hello, I'm Zainab Haidar, and i was born in 2003/12/31 in Ghazni province, in the district of Jaghori. I completed my school there and always had a deep passion for art. From a young age, I discovered my natural talent for drawing, and it quickly become one of my hobbies.I have always enjoyed creating beautiful works of art, and over time, I developed my skills further. During the free time, I immerse myself in creating art,as it brings me peace and serenity. When I paint, it's as if I disconnect from all the problems around me, and I find a sense of tranquility for my mind and soul. For me painting is more than just a hobby; it's a therapeutic journey that allows me to reconnect with myself.
